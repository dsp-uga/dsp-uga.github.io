CSCI 8360: Data Science Practicum

As a practicum, this course consists of a handful of group projects.

In each project, you and your team will employ data science and machine learning methods to answer a question using real-world datasets.
Meanwhile, the other teams will also be designing their own approaches.

Leaderboards will track the performance of each team's submission, scoring them against a hidden ground-truth.

Will your team submit the best model?
Will it be the best-designed code, best-documented project, and best-presented methods?
Can your team beat the all-time records for each project?

This class is not an introductory data science course.
This course assumes prior knowledge and experience in programming, statistics, and machine learning.
They're not requirements, but they are strongly suggested.
